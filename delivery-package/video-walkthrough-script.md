# Video Walkthrough Script (10 minutes)

1. **Intro (30s)**: Who you are, what the dashboard covers.
2. **Access (60s)**: How to open the dashboard, required email permissions.
3. **Overview Page (2m)**: KPIs, filters, date range controls.
4. **Platform Tabs (3m)**: GA4 events, HubSpot funnel, LinkedIn spend, Instantly sends/replies.
5. **Reconciliation Workbook (2m)**: Where to see variance notes and expected ranges.
6. **Maintenance (60s)**: Monthly checks, connector payments, who to contact.
7. **Next Steps (30s)**: How to request changes or add-ons.

Keep the recording concise; aim for 10 minutes total.
